How to Request Account Deletion

Users of Workforce Connect may request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

To request account deletion, please email:

Contact Us

Send your deletion request to:

workforceconnect@yahoo.com

Information to Include

Please include the following information in your email:

  • Full name
  • Email address associated with the account
  • Company name (if applicable)

What Happens Next

Once verified, the account and associated personal data will be permanently deleted within a reasonable timeframe, except where retention is required by law.

Note: Some records related to work history or legal compliance may be retained as required by applicable laws and regulations.
